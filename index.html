About

I am a software engineer with experience in building full-stack web applications using Javascript, Ruby on Rails, React/Redux, Node.js, PostgreSQL, SQL, and MongoDB. I'm a big believer in approaching challenges, whether in code or in life, with a strong sense of empathy, urgency, and optimism. As a software engineer, I'm driven by a desire for growth, excellence, and the empowerment of others through technology.

My coding philosophy is heavily influenced by these values as well as my prior experience in risk and project management. I calculate the associated costs or risks of potential solutions, and believe in the power of iterating towards a higher standard. In addition to creating applications with user-friendly designs and writing clean and maintainable code, I consider continuous improvement, effective communication, and great teamwork vital skills in my toolkit.

When not in front of my laptop, you'll likely find me enjoying local (and now virtual) dance classes or daydreaming about fulfilling a lifelong dream of getting a puppy.
